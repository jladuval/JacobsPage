Fluffy Link

Fluffy link was a project that went from conception to being live in under 48 hours. It was made as an alternative to tinyUrl for people who wanted shortened links (or even just nicer sounding links) to entice their friends visit other websites.

MVC4 Template

MVC4 Template was a template project that I created after my experience making a very large, highly structured social media website. With an emphasis on large scale architecture and modular web services the MVC4 template should help any developer begin any large project the right way.

About Me

I am a web developer from Christchurch, New Zealand. My experience is mainly with large scale, enterprise, web applications and I always strive to provide my clients with a stable and maintanable system. My passion for web development started shortly after I finished university and it has given me the drive to make the best applications that I possibly can. The web industry moves very quickly and I believe it is important to stay ahead of the curve in both front end technologies and back end coding.

I provide solutions ranging from personal home pages (much like this one) all the way up to enterprise and social media applications. I believe that visibility and communication are the most important considerations when creating a website and I try to make sure my clients are involved as much as possible. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions regarding a solution for you or your business, or just any web developement questions in general.
